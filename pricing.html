Questions

Asked plainly.

If something isn't here, ask it on the demo. We answer the question that was asked, not the one we wish you'd asked.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Twenty days, full features, no card. When it ends, the Free plan continues with one connected CRM and the core dashboard — you're never cut off cold.

How does seat pricing work?

Each plan includes a base number of users. Additional seats are $60 per user per month, prorated when you add or deactivate them — a freed seat credits your next invoice.

Which systems do you connect to?

GoHighLevel, Leap, AccuLynx, and JobNimbus for CRM; WhatConverts and CallRail for attribution and calls. Google Ads and accounting connectors are on the roadmap.

Do you write to my CRM?

No. TrueRead is read-only. We read, reconcile, and report — we never write, modify, or delete data in your systems.

What's the data-quality score?

A 0–100 composite across your connectors. Below 70, reports are flagged unreliable until the field gaps are filled — because a confident number built on bad data is worse than no number.

Do you resell our data?

No. The data belongs to your business. We use it to power your view of your business. That is the entire arrangement.
