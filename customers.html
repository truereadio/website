Four owners on what the picture looked like before, what they saw once it cleared, and the decision that came next.

owner portrait · b&w
Dale Wickerham
Owner

Wickerham Roofing

Residential roofing & exteriors$14M revenueToledo, OH
The problem

Three years of growth on the top line, two years of cash getting tighter — and no one could say exactly why.

The read

Labor variance on insurance jobs was running 18% above bid, and a single re-roof crew was absorbing the rest of the company's margin.

The outcome

Gross margin recovered from 26.4% to 32.1% in two quarters, without changing the price list.

owner portrait · b&w
Cara Vale
CEO

Mead & Vale Mechanical

Commercial HVAC & plumbing$31M revenueGreensboro, NC
The problem

Sales kept landing larger accounts; operations kept missing the timeline. Nobody owned the handoff data.

The read

Estimate-to-actual hours on projects over $200k were drifting 22% — but only on jobs sold by the two newest reps.

The outcome

Repricing the project tier and retraining two reps lifted project-side margin 5.8 points in nine months.

owner portrait · b&w
Pat Brennan
Founder

Brennan Bros. Exteriors

Siding, gutters, windows$7M revenueMadison, WI
The problem

Marketing spend had crept past $90k a month. No one could agree on which channels were actually working.

The read

Two paid channels were generating 42% of leads and 6% of booked revenue. The owner had been defending them in every team meeting.

The outcome

Cut $38k/month of paid spend. Booked revenue rose the following month — and the next two.

owner portrait · b&w
Sandra Liu
CFO

Highline Mechanical

Industrial process piping$48M revenueSalt Lake City, UT
The problem

End-of-year would surprise with a margin miss every December. The CFO had stopped trusting the forecast.

The read

Five long-running jobs were absorbing change orders that never reached billing. The leakage was traceable to a single workflow.

The outcome

Closed $2.1M of unbilled change orders. The forecast and the books agreed for the first time in four years.
